About us!

At George's Pizza Parlor, we’re all about bringing the authentic taste of Italy to your plate! Owned by George Baxter, a pizza aficionado with a deep love for Italian traditions, our parlor has earned a reputation for delicious, hand-crafted pizzas. Located in the heart of the city, we use only the finest ingredients, from our hand-tossed dough to fresh toppings, all based on time-honored family recipes. Whether it’s a classic Margherita or one of our specialty wood-fired pizzas, each bite is made with passion and care. Join us for an unforgettable meal, or order takeout to enjoy a slice of Italy at home!
